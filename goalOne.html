Goal One: Life Below Water

Protect and sustainably use the oceans, seas and marine resources for sustainable development

Over 3 billion people depend on marine and coastal biodiversity for their livelihoods. Yet, oceans are now more acidic than ever before, and plastic pollution is reaching epidemic proportions. Marine protected areas need to be effectively managed and well-resourced to ensure the survival of our planet's blue heart.

Protect Marine Biodiversity

We must protect endangered species such as sea turtles, coral reefs and marine mammals from overfishing, habitat destruction and climate change.

Learn More

Reduce Marine Pollution

80% of marine pollution comes from land-based activities. Reducing plastic use, proper waste management and sustainable consumption can reverse this trend.
